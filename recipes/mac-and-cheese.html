Simple Mac and Cheese

This is a quick and delicious version of an American classic made with sharp Cheddar. Add diced ham or tomatoes, or top with more cheese and broil for a crispy Cheddar crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Elbow Macaroni
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and reserve.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine milk, flour and salt and pepper to taste; whisk or beat until smooth. Add butter and cheese; microwave on high for 5 minutes and whisk until smooth. Microwave for an additional 4 to 5 minutes and whisk or beat until smooth and no lumps remain.
  3. Add cooked pasta to mixture; stir and serve.
